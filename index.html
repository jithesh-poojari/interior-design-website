Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A single room redesign typically takes 4-6 weeks, while full home renovations can take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your design fee structure?

We offer flexible pricing options including hourly rates, flat fees, and percentage-based fees. Our initial consultation is complimentary, and we'll provide a detailed proposal based on your specific project needs.

Do you work with existing furniture?

Absolutely! We're happy to incorporate your existing pieces into the new design. We'll assess what works with the new aesthetic and suggest updates or additions as needed.

Can you work within my budget?

Yes! We work with various budgets and will always be transparent about costs. We'll help you prioritize and make smart decisions to get the most impact from your investment.

Do you handle all aspects of the renovation?

We offer full-service project management, coordinating with contractors, ordering materials, and overseeing installation. You'll have a single point of contact throughout the entire process.
