Frequently Asked Questions

Here’s everything you might want to know before getting started

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on the size and scope of your space. A single room setup or makeover usually takes around 4–6 weeks, while complete home interiors may take 2–4 months. We’ll share a clear timeline after our first site visit.

What kind of projects do you take up?

We design cozy homes, apartments, and small commercial spaces such as cafés or offices. Most of our projects are for nuclear families or couples who want a warm, modern living space that feels personal.

Can you work within my budget?

Absolutely! We understand that every family has different needs and priorities. We’ll plan materials, furnishings, and finishes according to your budget while maintaining quality and style.

Do you work with local suppliers and contractors?

Yes, we collaborate with trusted local vendors and craftsmen in Manipal–Udupi to ensure smooth execution and timely delivery. This also helps us keep costs fair and support local businesses.

Can I include my existing furniture or décor?

Definitely! If you already have furniture or décor that holds sentimental value, we’ll happily integrate it into the new design. It’s about creating a home that feels truly yours.
